Who we are

We believe the social environment can influece the spirite of a community. UTOPIAN RAINBOW is a place which allow LGBTQ people to meet each other in real life, to have a deeper interactions while working together, and to build a tight community and strive for a better tomorrow together!


What's the problem we are solving?

According to the Psychology study, romantic relationships can be devided into three parts, sexual attraction, intimicy, and commitment. However, while cyber dating give people more choices online, it also give people little chance to know others deeper. People are more likly to compare each other, just like what we do in a supermarket. Therefore, people are more likely to have more sexual partners and less likly to make a commitment. However, due to the low population in society and social prejudice, most LGBTQ people have very little accessbility to know each others in real life. Dating apps became their only choice to socialize with others. This is the reason of the stereotype Why LGBTQ people are less likly to commit, while more likly to be depressed and feel lonely.
